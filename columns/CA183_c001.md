The Supreme Court, acting as the Presidential Electoral Tribunal, dismissed, 15-0, the election protest lodged by former senator Bongbong Marcos against VP Leni Robredo for “lack of merit.” The full text of the decision, dated Feb. 16, upholding Leni’s lawyers led by Romulo Macalintal, was released only a week ago though the dismissal was made public on the same date (Feb. 16).
