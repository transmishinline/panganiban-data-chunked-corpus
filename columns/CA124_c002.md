THE SUPREME COURT’S LATEST DECISION, Republic v. Bayog-Saito (Aug. 17, 2022) penned by J Henri Jean Paul B. Inting, recognized the divorce granted by the mayor of Minami-ku, Yokohama City, Japan, based merely on a “divorce notification” filed by the Japanese husband, Toru Saito, with the written acquiescence of his Filipino wife, Helen Bayog.
