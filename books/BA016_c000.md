# Restore Amla's Fangs
Date:       2010-07-11
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Opinion)
Source:     With Due Respect (2011)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban One sure way of catching big-time grafters is by tracing their loot, and then inquiring into and/or freezing their ill-gotten bank deposits. To achieve this goal, Congress approved the Anti-Money Laundering Act (Amla).
