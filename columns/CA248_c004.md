Nonetheless, the situation is far worse than I calculated by extrapolating from the previous statistics I knew. During my term as CJ, the Court had about 6,000 cases and we were already struggling to cope. In 10 years since then (2006-2016), the number has more than doubled! The 14,491 pending cases translate to an average of 966 (and counting) for each of the 15 justices.
