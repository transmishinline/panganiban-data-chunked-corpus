Alberto “Bert” David Lina may have passed to the Eternal Kingdom, but his legacy of love for his family, for his work, and for God will pervade this eulogy (that I edited and abridged to fit my limited space here). LET ME BEGIN WITH HIS LOVE FOR HIS FAMILY. Bert loved his wife Sylvia endearingly, tenderly, and eternally. She was the light of his life and the apple and mango of his eyes.
