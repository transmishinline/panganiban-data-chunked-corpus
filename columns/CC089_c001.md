The late Jaime Cardinal Sin is especially remembered for his indispensable role in calling and leading the faithful during the people power revolutions on Feb. 22-25, 1986 (Edsa 1) and on Jan. 16-20, 2001 (Edsa 2). Always propeace. As the archbishop of Manila, he held sway over more than 300 parishes and 70 parochial schools.
