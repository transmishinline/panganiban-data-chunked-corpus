The Judiciary Development Fund (JDF) Law (PD 1949) and the Special Allowance for the Judiciary (SAJ) Law (RA 9227) gives the CJ the "exclusive sole power" to disburse the JDF and SAJ funds. Our people look up to the CJ as an exemplar and role model. Because of our inquisitive media and open society, every public official is subjected to minute scrutiny. Thus, the CJ must withstand such scrutiny.
