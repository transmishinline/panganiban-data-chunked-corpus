Sadly, Congress did not heed the Court’s call for a new law. After I retired, the Court, in Banat v. Comelec (April 21, 2009), voided the 2-percent bar of the Party-list Law; and in Atong Paglaum v. Comelec (April 2, 2013), ruled that the party-list candidates and their nominees need not belong to the marginalized and underrepresented, thereby opening the party-list to everyone.
