Ah, them were the days when the bus fare on the Halili Transit and JD Bus was only 15 centavos; when four pesos could buy one US dollar; when we dated our girlfriends (who became our wives) at the Moonlit Terrace, Jai Alai Sky Room or Winter Garden; and when our favorite singers were Sylvia La Torre, Ruben Tagalog, Diomedes Maturan and Pilita Corrales.
