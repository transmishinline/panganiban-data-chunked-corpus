Time is either spelled out or in numbers. When in numbers, the abbreviations "a.m." or "p.m." (always in lower case) are used to indicate the time of day. e.g. eight o'clock in the morning or 8:00 a.m. ten thirty in the evening or 10:30 p.m. The term "o'clock" is used only with exact hours that are spelled out; not when the minutes are also indicated or when figures are used. 6.
