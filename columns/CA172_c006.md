Registered political parties, like the PDP-Laban, are required to file with the Comelec their “Sworn Information and Update Statement” (SIUS) on or before Aug. 15, a requirement that both “factions” claim to have fulfilled. Consequently, the Comelec is legally tasked, after due process, to select the valid SIUS. Should the Comelec fail to decide in the next 30 days, it must do so after Oct.
