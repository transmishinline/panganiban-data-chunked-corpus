The newly minted lawyer, however, looked up to certain legal luminaries to whom history would later ascribe places of honor\. They became guideposts whom he sought to emulate\. Foremost among them was Dean Jovito R\. Salonga and, fortuitously, Art had come under his mentorship while he was still a law student\. Following graduation from the FEU, Art was heady over finishing in the top 10\.
