The dissolution orphaned my law partners, Dean Agustin Benitez (topnotcher in the 1959 Bar exam), Dean Custodio Parlade (fourth in the same exam), and my classmate Leopoldo Africa (11th in 1960) but they fully understood my action. Please excuse my feeble attempts to imitate these legends of the bar as I humbly try, though undeserving, to live in the penumbra of their great shadows.
