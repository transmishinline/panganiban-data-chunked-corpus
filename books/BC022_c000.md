# The Bio-Age Dawns on the Judiciary -- Preface
Source:     The Bio-Age Dawns on the Judiciary (book)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban PREFACE As my small contribution to transparency, I try to report on my work as a member of the Supreme Court yearly, in book form. The present volume covers the salient events during my eighth year in office, from October 10, 2002 to October 10, 2003.
