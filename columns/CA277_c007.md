Having invalidated DOJ 41, Justice Reyes was not unmindful of the "dilemma of the DOJ" in preventing the flight of suspects particularly in capital offenses. The solution, according to him, is "for the legislature … to identify permissible intrusions in the right to travel" and for the DOJ to exercise "more vigilance and efficiency."
