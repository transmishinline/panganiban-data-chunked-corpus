Through the years, I have been truly blessed to have the opportunity to meet and befriend many Bedan lawyers, all brilliant and distinguished. In the National Union of Students of the Philippines in the late fifties and when I was a young lawyer in the early sixties, I met talented, up-and-coming men like Raul Roco, Rene Saguisag, Pablo Trillana, Rudy Robles, Vic de la Serna and Boy Brillantes.
