All our Constitutions – starting from our 1897 Biak na Bato Constitution to the 1898 Malolos Constitution, the 1935, 1973 and 1987 Constitutions – were born amid our people's struggle for political independence. Understandably, the Bills of Rights in all of them contain our people's aspirations to be free of external or internal forces that stifle our freedom to think, to speak and to act.
