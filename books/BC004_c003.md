I am especially touched by the phrase "meek and humble of heart" because it describes exactly what Pat was, meek and humble of heart. I met Pat, along with Ben Pangilinan, when they were both introduced to me by the late Mars Quimson in 1987. As legal counsel to their "Excel Group," I was ushered by Pat, Ben, and Mars to the Philippine Daily Inquirer and to Marixi and A Prieto.
