Inting, Antonio T. Kho Jr. (with Separate Opinion), and Rey E. Bulay—unanimously dismissed the petition “seeking to deny due course to and/or cancel the Certificate of Candidacy (COC)…” of Bongbong Marcos (BBM). FALSE MATERIAL REPRESENTATION is the exclusive ground to cancel a COC. “There is no other possible ground,” the Division firmly ruled, and I agree.
