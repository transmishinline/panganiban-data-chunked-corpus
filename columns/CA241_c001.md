The Top 10 places in the last (2018) bar exams were captured by three Ateneo de Manila law graduates (Top 1, 4 and 5), four by the University of San Carlos (Top 2, 3, 9 and 10), two by the University of the Philippines (Top 6 and 7) and one by the De La Salle University (Top 8). That’s the good news. The bad news is that only 1,800 or 22.07 percent of the 8,155 who took the tests passed.
