In a past column, Ciel Habito advised separating the micros from the small and medium enterprises; however, for the anti-corruption study, the AIM lumped them together simply as “SMEs.” Altogether, SMEs (including the micros) constitute 99.6 percent of all businesses, employ 63.2 percent of the labor force, and account for 35.7 percent of total sales in our country. Victims of petty graft.
