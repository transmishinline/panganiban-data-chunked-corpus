“HAVE FAITH IN GOD.” During my own season of passion and death, I moved among the high and the lowly, the powerful and the weak, the glitterati and the downtrodden. I was outwardly active, yet inwardly hollow. My restless pursuits brought me no peace, no fulfillment, no sense of purpose. My turning point came in 1986, when I found Jesus Christ and was baptized in the Holy Spirit.
