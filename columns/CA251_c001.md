To set the record straight at the outset, Maria Ressa was indicted by the Department of Justice (DOJ) for the "republication" by Rappler of an allegedly libelous online article on Feb. 19, 2014, not for its original posting on May 29, 2012. In fact, the DOJ's Resolution, dated Jan. 10, 2019, exonerated Ressa from libel arising from the original posting.
