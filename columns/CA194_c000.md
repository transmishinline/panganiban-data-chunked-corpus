# Can presidents pardon themselves? Date:       2020-12-20
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Inquirer Opinion)
Source:     https://opinion.inquirer.net/136289/can-presidents-pardon-themselves
By: Artemio V. Panganiban | 05:04 AM December 20, 2020 In the Philippines, the answer is certainly “No,” because a pardon may be granted only after a conviction by a final judgment.
