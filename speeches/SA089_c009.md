Outnumbered, the Spanish cazadores, led by Capitan Enrique de las Morenas, retreated to the stone-walled Baler parish church. Despite deficiencies in supplies, food and occasional attacks by the Katipuneros, the Spanish troops held courageously on for 11 months, or to be exact, 337 days. ## END OF THE SIEGE With this inevitable ending, the Spanish cazadores surrendered on June 2, 1899.
