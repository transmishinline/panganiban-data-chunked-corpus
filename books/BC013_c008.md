In January 1995, after having been bypassed seven times, and convinced that the Lord did not want me to be in the Supreme Court, I humbled myself and bowed to what I thought was God's will for me. My spirit was shattered and my heart bled, but I uplifted my ambition to God with a solemn prayer: Take, O Lord, all that I am, all that I will ever be. Take my personal ambition, my liberty, my career.
