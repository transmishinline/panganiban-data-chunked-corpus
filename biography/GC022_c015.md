“I would think of my schoolmates as they slept on their beds, simply resting and letting the hours pass until it was time to wake up for school\. But I had to work at an early age to be able to go to school\. I would tell myself, “Today I am just a newsboy\. Someday, I will be president of the *Manila Times*\. It was then the largest\-circulated daily in the country\.”
