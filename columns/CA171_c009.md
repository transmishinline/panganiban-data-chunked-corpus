Republic (Jan 18, 2017), unanimously affirmed a Sandiganbayan decision which “labeled as ill-gotten and forfeited” in favor of the Republic “the pieces of jewelry, known as the Malacañang Collection.” CJ Sereno penned the decision, concurred in by JJs Del Castillo, Reyes, Perlas-Bernabe, and Caguioa; issued during the presidency of Rodrigo Roa Duterte.
