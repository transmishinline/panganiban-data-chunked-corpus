Yearly since 2017, the Foundation for Liberty and Prosperity (FLP), in partnership with the Tan Yan Kee Foundation and the Ayala Corporation, awards scholarships at P200,000 each to at least 20 law students, and cash prizes in its dissertation writing contest (P320,000 first prize, P220,000 second prize, P120,000 for each of three third prizes, and P20,000 each to a max of 20 finalists).
