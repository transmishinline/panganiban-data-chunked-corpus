She opened her house to so many of our friends; was always the cool mom, or should I say, warm and welcoming “Tita Leni” or “Mommy Not” to some. In true soft Mom Leni fashion, she raised the five of us with quiet authority. She wasn’t overbearing, she wasn’t strict; she wasn’t in my face. Always patient. Always kind. Always wise. She said eight was the age of reason, so she just reasoned with us.
