The PCA has acted as Registry in 11 out of 12 arbitration cases filed under Annex VII of Unclos. Enforcement. The award on jurisdiction, dated Oct. 29, 2015, held that despite its nonparticipation, China “is bound by any award the Tribunal issues.” However, the Tribunal has no power to enforce its awards. It does not have any sheriff, policeman, or soldier to compel obedience to its orders.
