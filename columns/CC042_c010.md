A lot of my barkada now still remember Lola and those days in Dasma fondly … She taught me how to be patient, kind, and caring. She taught me how to curl my tongue, play Uno, Old Maid, Sudoku, but she couldn’t teach me how to raise both my eyebrows, I never figured out that one. She also had the coolest phones. She loved having nice phones back then, it was her treat to herself.
