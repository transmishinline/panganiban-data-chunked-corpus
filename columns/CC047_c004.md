PERLAS-BERNABE will retire on May 14, ending her over 25-year ascent in the judicial ladder via her appointments as Metropolitan Trial Court Judge of Makati in 1996 by Fidel V. Ramos; as Regional Trial Court Judge also of Makati in 2000 by Joseph Estrada; as Court of Appeals Justice in 2004 by Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo; and as Supreme Court Justice in 2011 by Benigno Aquino III.
