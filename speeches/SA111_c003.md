To the question of which president is the most corrupt in the history of the Philippines, a Pulse Asia survey revealed: Gloria Arroyo, 42 percent; Ferdinand Marcos, 35 percent; Joseph Estrada, 16 percent; Fidel Ramos, 5 percent; Corazon Aquino, 1 percent. A staggering 71 percent of business managers said they had been blatantly asked for bribes in connection with their dealings with government.
