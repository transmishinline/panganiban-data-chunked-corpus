FOURTH, JESUS IS A LEADER OF INTENSE PRAYER AND UNBENDING FAITH. The Bible is replete with stories of Jesus praying. Just before he walked on the water, the evangelist Matthew related that “He went up on a mountain by Himself to pray, remaining there alone (Matthew 14:23-24).” In the garden of Gethsemane, Jesus prayed so intensely that his sweat became like drops of blood falling to the ground.
