# Can the Senate Stop a GMA Holdover? Date:       2010-02-07
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Opinion)
Source:     With Due Respect (2011)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban To stop 30 President Macapagal-Arroyo from prolonging her stay, a senator whose term expires only in 2013 should be elected Senate president. This was the near unanimous reaction of readers to last Sunday's column.
