# 'Hindi Puede ang Puede na'
Date:       2010-04-18
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Opinion)
Source:     With Due Respect (2011)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban The Commission on Elections is preparing for the manual count of 30 percent of the ballots, in case the PCOS machines snag. This was affirmed by, of all people, Defense Secretary Norberto Gonzales.
