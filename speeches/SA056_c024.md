He has served the judiciary for almost a quarter of a century: in the Court of Appeals (2003-2017) and in the Regional Trial Court of Quezon City (1994 to 2003). He finished his law degree cum laude and as salutatorian of the San Beda College law class of 1971. President Duterte was his batch mate and Justice Secretary Vit Aguirre, his valedictorian.
