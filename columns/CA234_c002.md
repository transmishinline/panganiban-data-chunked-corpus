So ruled the Supreme Court in its very first sentence in Kwong v Diamond Homeowners (June 10, 2019), penned by Justice Marvic M. V. F. Leonen. Specifically, the Court upheld the “No Sticker, No ID, No Entry” policy of gated villages. Under this scheme, visitors on vehicles seeking entry must leave with the subdivision guards their identification cards (IDs), which they may reclaim upon leaving.
