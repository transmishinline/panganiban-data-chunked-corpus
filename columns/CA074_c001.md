The Supreme Court is planning to require all lawyers to render at least 60 hours of pro bono (or free) legal aid services to the poor every three years during their professional career under its new Unified Legal Aid Services (ULAS) program. THE COURT CREATED A TECHNICAL WORKING GROUP (TWG) with Justice Alfredo Benjamin S. Caguioa as chair and Justice Rodil V. Zalameda as vice chair.
