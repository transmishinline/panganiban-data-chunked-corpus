In concluding His sermon on the mount, Jesus Christ exhorted us: "Be perfect, just as Your heavenly Father is perfect." (Mt.5:48) By this message of Christ, Christians are called to be holy. The 1987 Synod of Bishops stated that through baptism "a person is called to a life of holiness." That is why in BLD (Bukas Loob sa Diyos), holiness is the first and foremost community value.
