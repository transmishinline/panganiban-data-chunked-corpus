Romans, Chapter 14, Verse 17 says that "(t)he kingdom of God is not a matter of eating or drinking, but of justice, p e a c e d and the joy that is given by the Holy Spirit." Justice, f. peace and joy. These are hallmarks of Christ. When the bible speaks of justice, it really refers to righteousness — being "right" with God. We are right with God when we obey His commandments and follow His ways.
