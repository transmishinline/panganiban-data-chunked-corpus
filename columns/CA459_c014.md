Legal reasons abound allowing the OSG to intervene and to have the PBA nullified. At the very least, the Constitution and the Ombudsman Act (RA 6770) authorize the Ombudsman to “request any government agency for assistance… in the discharge of [her] responsibilities…” Notably, the Ombudsman’s position paper also prayed that the OSG “be allowed to extend all needful help or aid to this office…”
