As I explained last Sunday, although the proposed Bangsamoro Basic Law (BBL) changed the word “associative” to “asymmetric,” still the MILF’s lofty aspiration for self-determination remains unchanged. Notably, the MILF’s chief negotiator for the old MOA-AD and for the new Comprehensive Agreement on the Bangsamoro (CAB) upon which the BBL is anchored is one and the same person: Mohagher Iqbal.
