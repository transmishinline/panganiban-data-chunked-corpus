So, when I returned home, I spoke with Bert and suggested that he add nine more bells to complete the 14 existing bells to 23. But he remonstrated, No, Ninong, I will not give nine bells. I will give a complete new set of 23 bells because, if I just add nine new ones, they may not be in tune with the existing 14. I must give the best to our Cathedral. Church bells are the voice of God.
