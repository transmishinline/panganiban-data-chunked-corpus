Ably written by Justice Marvic M.V.F. Leonen, the decision debunked the three causes of action raised by Bongbong’s lawyers led by George Garcia. The first cause to nullify the Certificates of Canvass for being allegedly “not authentic” was thrown out during the Pre-Trial Conference on Aug. 29, 2017. The second cause involved the revision and recount of the ballots in 36,465 clustered precincts.
