The young man saw and described him as a dedicated husband and caring father, a faithful friend and a devoted man of God who did not smoke or drink or gamble\. A well\-regarded leader of the Cosmopolitan Church, Dr\. Salonga lived a simple, almost ascetic life\. Wealth, power, worldly allurements, titles and honors did not fascinate him\. Art saw him as a model for how to live meaningfully\.
