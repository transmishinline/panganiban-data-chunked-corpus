According to the Comelec, 81.62 percent, or about 40 million of the 54.4 million registered voters, cast their ballots. These figures are better than the turnout of 77 percent in 2013 and 74.8 percent in 2010. Noteworthy, too, is the fact that, per Comelec records, 95 percent of the results were transmitted electronically within 17 hours after the polling centers closed on Election Day.
