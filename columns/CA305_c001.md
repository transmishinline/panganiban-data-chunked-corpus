The 14 separate opinions of the magistrates in Lagman vs Medialdea (July 4, 2017) may be indigestible legal gobbledygook for lay people, but they constitute a veritable legal feast for law scholars. They will be relevant when the putative challenges to the extension of Proclamation No. 216 (P216), the martial law (ML) edict, are reviewed. Concurring opinions.
