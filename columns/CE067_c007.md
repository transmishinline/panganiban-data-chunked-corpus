And the Comelec itself admitted that 961 out of 85,769 VCMs and 1,665 out of 85,960 secure digital (SD) cards malfunctioned. Whatever the correct numbers are, one glitch is enough to disenfranchise a citizen. Former VP Jojo Binay’s ballot was rejected eight times by a VCM. He had to complain personally at the Comelec office before he was able to exercise his constitutional right to vote.
