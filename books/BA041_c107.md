The penalty imposed must be commensurate to the depravity of the malfeasance, violation or crime being punished. A grave injustice is committed in the name of justice when the penalty imposed is grossly disproportionate to the wrong committed. (*The Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation v. NLRC and Meneses, *G.R. No. 116542, July 30, 1996, 260 SCRA 49) **I.
