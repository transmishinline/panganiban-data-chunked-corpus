Relevant to Revilla, three petitions for certiorari have been lodged in the Supreme Court; two were filed by Revilla questioning (1) the OOO’s finding of probable cause, and (2) the SBN’s order denying him bail; and one was filed by the OOO seeking the transfer of Revilla from the PNP Custodial Center to Camp Bagong Diwa in Taguig.
