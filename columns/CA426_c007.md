24, 2006, then President Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo issued Presidential Proclamation 1017 declaring a “state of national emergency” that resulted in, among others, the warrantless arrest of many, including columnist Randy David and Akbayan president Roland Llamas, as well as in the pillage of the Tribune and the Malaya. A week later, on March 3, 2006, President Arroyo lifted PP 1017.
