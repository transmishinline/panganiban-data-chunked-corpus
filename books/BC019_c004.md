Prior to his appointment to the Sup was national vice chairman of the PPreme Court, he CRV, secretary of the Council of the Laity of the Philippines and a servant-leader of the Bukas Loob sa Diyos Covenant Community. He was also a lay delegate to the Second Plenary Council of the Philippines (PCP II) held in January to February 1991. PCP II is the Church equivalent of a constitutional convention.
