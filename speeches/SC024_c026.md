Michael Nolan, who taught me four basic prayers, the "Our Father," the "Hail Mary," the "Glory Be" and the FEU Prayer which I can still recite from memory up to now: "Direct, O God, we beseech Thee all our actions by Thy holy inspiration and help us on by Thy gracious assistance, so that every prayer and work of ours may begin with Thee and by Thee be happily ended.
