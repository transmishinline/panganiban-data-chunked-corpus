New justices take the bulk of the cases. When I entered the Court in 1995, I was assigned over 1,000 cases. I had to work day and night to reduce my caseload, such that upon my retirement on Dec. 6, 2006, I left only about 200 cases, all of them current. In addition to the 6,000 pending cases, another 6,000 new ones are filed annually.
