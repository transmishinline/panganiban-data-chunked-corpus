"The dichotomy made in the ponencia between 'voluntary renunciation of the office' as used in Section 8 of Article X of the Constitution and Section 43(b) of R.A. 7160 and involuntary severance from office is unnecessary, if not misplaced. From the discussion in the ponencia, the latter is made to apply to the banned term, i.e., the fourth term immediately following three consecutive terms.
