# 11 SC seats to fill

By: Artemio V. Panganiban | 05:30 AM September 10, 2017 During the 6-year term of President Duterte (2016-2022), 12 of the 15 seats in the Supreme Court will be vacated as the incumbents reach the compulsory retirement age of 70.
