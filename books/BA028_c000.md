# Not Easy to be a Cabinet Member
Date:       2010-06-20
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Opinion)
Source:     With Due Respect (2011)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban Many are jockeying for the choicest positions in the incoming government. These job aspirants should however realize that it would not be easy to serve in the Cabinet of President Noynoy Aquino.
