Ricardo Alfonso. Medical interventions. In 1983, I was told by Dr. Augusto Litonjua, “I am sorry to say you have diabetes. This is incurable but can be controlled by diet, exercise and a little medicine.” I followed his prescription to the letter. And voila! In 1995, prior to my entry into the Supreme Court, Dr. Litonjua formally certified that my blood sugar had normalized. My diabetes was gone!
