Together with President Fidel V. Ramos, I was invited to speak "about the book and the author." **Shadowy and enigmatic** I met the author, fondly called JoeAl, just before FVR took office on June 30, 1992. Initially, I was wary of him. Because of his well-publicized deep-penetration exploits, I thought of him as some sort of James Bond and Napoleon Solo.
