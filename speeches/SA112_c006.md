## MOST CORRUPT IN HISTORY More tellingly, our own people regard the Arroyo administration as the most corrupt in our history. To the question of which president is the most corrupt in the history of the Philippines, the answers were: Gloria Arroyo, 42 percent; Ferdinand Marcos, 35 percent; Joseph Estrada, 16 percent; Fidel Ramos, 5 percent; Corazon Aquino, 1 percent.
