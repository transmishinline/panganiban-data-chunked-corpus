Due to the recent appointments, the Court's three divisions have been reorganized as follows: First Division, Sereno (chair), De Castro, Del Castillo, Jardeleza, and Tijam; Second Division, Carpio (chair), Peralta, Bernabe, Caguioa and Reyes; and Third Division, Velasco (chair), Bersamin, Leonen, Martires and Gesmundo. More than 80 percent of all cases are decided by the divisions.
