Specifically, CJH DevCo was ordered to return the 247-hectare portion (Leased Property) out of the total 625-hectare John Hay Special Economic Zone to the BCDA. In turn, the BCDA was directed to refund to CJH DevCo the sum of P1,421,096,052 (P1.4 billion) paid as rental to the former by the latter. In short, it restored the parties to the status quo ante bellum.
