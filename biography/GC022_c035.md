Under “Ambition,” I indicated that I wanted to be a chemical engineer\. He took me aside and spoke to me seriously\. He asked that I, his youngest son, should do what he had wanted to do for himself, which was to become a lawyer\. Since I carried his name, I would be fulfilling his unrealized dream\. Besides, he added that I was a *pilosopo* who tended to argue with him and my siblings\.
