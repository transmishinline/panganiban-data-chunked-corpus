Henry Sy more than fifty years ago, when as a struggling student, I bought my shoes at his Shoemart store in Carriedo Street in Manila at, believe it or not, only ten pesos at that time. His store progressed because he sold the best pairs at the most reasonable prices. From Carriedo, he expanded to the Makati Commercial Center, to North EDSA, thence all over. Shoemart metamorphosed into SM.
