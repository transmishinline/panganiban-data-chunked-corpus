Without obedience, everything is useless in God's eyes. It would be of no use starting to work if we did not intend to count on our Lord. Even our most worthwhile endeavors would be fruitless if we did not have the desire to carry out God's will." (Fr. Francis Fernandez, On Conversation with God, Vol. Three, p. 235) As St. Crysostom teaches, "God does not need our work. He needs our obedience."
