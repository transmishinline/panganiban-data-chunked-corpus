Every summer, my wife and I spend two weeks in out-of-the-way places, like Machu Picchu in Peru, Huangshan (Yellow Mountains) in China, the mammoth Iguassu Falls straddling Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay or the Canadian Rockies. But this year, I had to cut short our two-week journey into the wilderness of Alaska (and a side trip to New York to visit our apos) to attend the Liturgy Congress.
