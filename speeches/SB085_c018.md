The original decision of the Supreme Court issued on January 27, 2004, by vote of 8-5, struck down as unconstitutional the major provisions of the Mining Law, the IRR and the FTAA. However, on reconsideration, the Court on December 1, 2004 reversed itself by a more definitive vote of 10-4. It vigorously and fully upheld the constitutionality of the Mining Law, the IRR and the FTAA.
