I had to walk in knee-deep flood water to the University of the East where the exams were being held. When the results came out, I still managed to place 6th. As for my personal career, I got to the Supreme Court without really aspiring for it. One day in June 1992, the then newly-elected president of our country, Fidel V. Ramos, invited me to a one-on-one meeting in his private office in Makati.
