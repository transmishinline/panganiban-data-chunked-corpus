I, of course, fondly remember that when I was the incumbent Chief Justice of my country, Justice Chao was the incumbent ALA President and that he, together with several ALA VIPs from other Asean countries, flew to the Philippines to honor me with a big celebration at the nearby Dusit Thane Hotel. Thank you indeed, Justice Chao. I will never forget the honor you bestowed on me as long as I live.
