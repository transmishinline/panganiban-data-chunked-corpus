Miguel handed JP II his colored drawing titled “Viva il Papa” and a salakot, which the Pontiff gamely wore for a while and then waved joyfully to the crowd. Vada gifted him with a garland and a poem she had composed. In 1996, JP II named me a member for five years of the Pontifical Council for the Laity (PCL), which met with him annually in the Vatican.
