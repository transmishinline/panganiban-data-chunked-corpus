Romulo was the initial VP choice of President Manuel Roxas (Mar's grandfather). But Romulo begged off, preferring to be ambassador to the United States (See Romulo, Crusade in Asia, p. 16). With the untimely demise of Roxas in 1948, the unheralded Quirino inherited the man tle. The colorful Manila Mayor Arsenio H. Lacson was President Ramon Magsaysay's preferred teammate.
