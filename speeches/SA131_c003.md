Yesterday, at the Philippine International Convention Center, 1,893 law graduates who passed the 2006 bar examinations were sworn in as new members of the noblest profession in the world. The Supreme Court ended its summer session in Baguio and the justices motored to Manila to attend the solemn rites. A graduate of the University of the Cordilleras (Noel Neil Q. Malimba) topped the test.
