To qualify, applicants must: 1) be third year law students in an eligible school, 2) belong to the top 10 percent of their class, 3) have an average of at least 83 percent (or 2.25) for the four prior semesters, 4) have full loads with no subject dropped, 5) submit a written commendation of their dean, 6) write an essay on liberty and prosperity, and 7) pass the screening of the board of judges.
