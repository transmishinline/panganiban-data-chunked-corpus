"Young lady," solemnly intoned His Honor, "this police officer says you were driving at 100 kms. per hour in a 50-kilometer zone. What do you have to say?" "Your Honor," grieved the young lady, "I admit my mistake. I am truly sorry and I solemnly promise I will not do it again." "For speeding, you are hereby sentenced to pay a fine of P500," concluded the judge.
