In the end, eight justices (Puno, Hilario Davide Jr., Florenz Regalado, Flerida Ruth Romero, Josue Bellosillo, Jose Vitug, Artemio Panganiban and Antonio Martinez) voted to invalidate AO 308 while six (Chief Justice Andres Narvasa, Justices Jose Melo, Santiago Kapunan, Mendoza, Leonardo Quisumbing and Fidel Purisima) dissented.
