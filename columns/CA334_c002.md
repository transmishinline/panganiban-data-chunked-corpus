Last July 25, within his first month in office, Commissioner Dulay issued Revenue Memorandum Order (RMO) 44-2016 excluding these nonstock, nonprofit schools (NNS) from the coverage of an earlier order (RMO 20-2013) issued on July 22, 2013. RMO 20-2013 lumped these NNS with corporations and associations which are granted tax exemptions by the National Internal Revenue Code (NIRC).
