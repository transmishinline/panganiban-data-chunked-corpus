At his arraignment, Gervacio entered a plea of guilty “deliberately and with full knowledge of its effects.” Thus, the SBN Second Division (composed of Justices Teresita V. Diaz-Baldos, chair, Napoleon E. Inoturan and Maria Cristina J. Cornejo) applied the “mitigating circumstance of plea of guilty” and sentenced him to a “straight penalty of four months and one day” in jail. Probation explained.
