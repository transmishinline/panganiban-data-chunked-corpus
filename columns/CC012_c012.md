Whenever either of them was on the opposite side, he would caution me as his then scrabbling assistant, “Please prepare our trial brief extra well because Pepe Diokno (or Dingdong Teehankee) is our opponent.”
Teehankee had impeccable academic credentials: summa cum laude in both his Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws from Ateneo de Manila, and numero uno in the 1940 Bar exam.
