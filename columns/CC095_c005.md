In addition, a married woman, according to the Civil Code, may use “(1) her maiden first name and surname and add her husband’s surname, or (2) her maiden first name and her husband’s surname, or (3) her husband’s full name, but prefixing a word indicating that she is his wife, such as ‘Mrs.’” Example: My wife may use (1) Elenita Carpio Panganiban, or (2) Elenita Panganiban, or (3) Mrs.
