Doy Laurel saw the future in him. On his deathbed, he asked his nephew and political chieftain Jose Macario Laurel to recruit Villar into the Nacionalista Party. Incidentally, Doy Laurel wanted the presidency too. But like his father, Jose P. Laurel, who gave way to Magsaysay in 1953, Doy withdrew in favor of Cory in the 1986 snap election.
