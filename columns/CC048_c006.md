I had skips in my heart rhythm, failed the ECG, the 2D echocardiography, and the treadmill stress tests but, thank God, passed the angiogram exam. Dr. Dante Morales assured me that my heart arteries were okay and that I had a rare, natural skip in my heart beats. In 1949, while peddling newspapers aboard the rear of a speeding jeepney, I jumped and lost my balance.
