I first met EJA, Ed, or Edong in 1979 when he was elected president of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines. I joined him when he organized and became the founding president of the Asean Law Association (or ALA) in 1980. Since then, I have continuously attended ALA activities. In fact, ALA, where I am the current chair of the Philippine chapter, is the only law organization I am still active in.
