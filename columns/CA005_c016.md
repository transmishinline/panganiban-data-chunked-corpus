Under the new SC resolution, if the basic pay had been increased to P30,000, then the first P20,000 would still be sourced from the national government, while the pay increase of P10,000 would be taken from the SAJ. In addition, the original non-taxable SAJ of P20,000 will still be given from the SAJ. In short, the judge will have a base pay of P30,000 plus a SAJ of P20,000.
