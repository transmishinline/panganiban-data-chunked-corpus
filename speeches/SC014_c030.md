The Program of Action, a "first" in RCM's history, called for a budget of P90 million, an outlay that was not twice, not thrice, but 18 times more than the biggest budget of P5 million that RCM had in the past. During my inaugural address on July 12, 1990, I outlined these 178 projects. The members were aghast at the huge budget and the ambitious Program of Action.
