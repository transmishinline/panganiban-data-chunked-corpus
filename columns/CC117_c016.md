To show that He was ready to perform the humblest of service, He washed the feet of His apostles, telling them, “You address me as ‘Teacher’ and ‘Lord’ and fittingly enough for that is what I am. But if I, who am Teacher and Lord, have washed your feet, then you, too, must wash each other’s feet. What I did was to give you an example: as I have done for you, so you must do.” (Jn 13:14-16)
