We were “An Army of Youth” that fought for Christ the King in the campuses everywhere. Indefatigable trio. To celebrate this SCA milestone, three indefatigable ladies—Edna Z. Manlapaz, Imelda M. Nicolas and Teresita C. Villacorta—invited the SCA alumni to write down and share their fondest remembrances of those 75 years. A large number from all over the planet—52 to be exact—responded.
