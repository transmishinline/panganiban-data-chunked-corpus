There were several life\-sized religious statues which the noted Quiapo *santonero* Maximo Vicente had been commissioned to make\. Among these were the Sacred Heart of Jesus, St\. Joseph with Jesus, and Our Lady of Fatima\. Years later, National Artist for Sculpture Napoleon Abueva would donate a fiber\-glass sculpture of *La Pieta,* inspired by Michelangelo’s masterpiece in Rome\.
