Empowering the poor through education and entrepreneurship is precisely what the Jollibee Group Foundation (JGF) has been doing via two projects called “Busog, Lusog, Talino (BLT)” and “Farmers Entrepreneurship Program (FEP).” During the JGF’s 10th-anniversary celebration days ago, its president Grace Tan Caktiong noted that the state provides free elementary and high school education.
