38. 9.3 Records/Rollo a. The page number/s in the records or rollo , as the case may be, of the cited material (usually a pleading, a decision, an order, or a documentary evidence) is indicated. e.g. Rollo, pp. 2-20. b. Where the cited material is found in both the rollo and the records of the case, both may be indicated but separated by a semicolon.
