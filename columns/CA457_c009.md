If the BIR rejects the application, or fails to act on it within 120 days, the applicant has 30 days to appeal to the Court of Tax Appeals (CTA). Otherwise, the VAT refund would be forfeited. San Roque filed its refund application with the BIR on March 28, 2003. But even before the lapse of the 120 days, it filed a judicial claim in the CTA on April 10, 2003.
