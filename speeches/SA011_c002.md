Dean Gil, why did you apologize for rendering the Invocation? I think we should be proud that we pray. To be clear, praying is not disallowed by any law; in fact, it is encouraged by our Constitution and by our Supreme Court. As we all know, the Preamble of our Constitution begins with "We, the sovereign Filipino people, imploring the aid of Almighty God . . ." That's an express prayer to God.
