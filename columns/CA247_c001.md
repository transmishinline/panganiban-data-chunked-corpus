Readers ask if the congressional bill creating the position of "judges-at-large" will help clear the dockets of our trial courts. First, let us review the statistics. As of 2017, over 640,000 cases pended in the 1,100 Regional Trial Courts (RTCs) all over the country, or an average of 580 cases per sala. That is just too many to handle even for the brightest and most diligent magistrates.
