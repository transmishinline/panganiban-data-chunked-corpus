To be a lawyer was my strong-willed father's impossible dream. So, he overruled my own preference for chemical engineering and insisted on a law career for me, the youngest of the brood. But I could not enjoy my scholarship at the University of the Philippines because I could not afford the bus fare to the UP campus in Quezon City. "Diyan ka sa FEU. Hindi na kailangan ang pamasahe," he told me.
