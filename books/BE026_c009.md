Indeed, intelligence, crime proclivity and sexuality - three higher-level behaviors with genetic roots - may be enhanced or depressed via manipulations of the brain. Already, there are brand-name medicines that alter human conduct by re-circuiting neurotransmitters in the brain. Some of these are Prozac, manufactured by Eli Lilly; Zoloft, by Pfizer; and Paxil by Smithkline Beecham.
