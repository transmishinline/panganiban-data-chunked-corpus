Their eldest was a daughter named Teresita\. She was followed by Nardo and then Lolita\. The youngest son and namesake, Artemio Villaseñor Panganiban Jr\., was born on December 7, 1936 at the Mary Johnston Hospital in Tondo\. A year prior to the young Artemio’s entry into the world, Manila had just been designated as the capital of the newly formed Commonwealth of the Philippines\.
