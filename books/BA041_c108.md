On Promoting Cultural Values and Religious Faith ** Just a word edgewise about cultural values. Our cultural ideals and core values of *galang, pagbabahala, pananagutan, balikatan, malasakit, asal, halaga, diwa, damdamin, dangal, kapwa, pakikitungo, hiya, delikadesa, awa, tiwala, makaDiyos, maka-tao, maka-buhay* and so forth define us as a people, as Filipinos.
