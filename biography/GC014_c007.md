That day, he prayed again. “If this is truly Your will,” he asked quietly, “give me another sign. One more. I do not want to be deceived by my own longing.” One week later, in the same church, with the same priest, the Gospel was read again—this time the account of the **Transfiguration**. *A cloud came and overshadowed them… and a voice said:* *This is my **Son**, my Chosen One.*
