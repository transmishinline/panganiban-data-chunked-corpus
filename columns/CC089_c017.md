Upon seeing me, he whispered, “We have won!” Just as Cardinal Sin retired in 2003, the archdiocese of Manila was reduced to about a fourth of its original size. Five dioceses (Caloocan, Cubao, Novaliches, Parañaque and Pasig) were carved out from it; only the cities of Manila, Makati, Mandaluyong, Pasay and San Juan remained.
