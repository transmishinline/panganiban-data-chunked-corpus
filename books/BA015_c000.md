# Divorce, Pinoy Style
Date:       2010-02-21
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Opinion)
Source:     With Due Respect (2011)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban A Western-style absolute divorce between spouses who are both Filipinos even if obtained abroad—is not valid here. Although divorce is taboo, our courts can nonetheless void a marriage due to a fatal defect at its inception.
