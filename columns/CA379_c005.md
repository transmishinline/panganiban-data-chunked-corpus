As a foundling found in Jaro, Iloilo, she acquired the domicile (and citizenship) of her parents who, according to “generally-accepted principles of law,” are presumed to be Filipinos. So, her domicile of origin is Jaro, Iloilo. After she married an American and moved to and worked in the United States, she lost her domicile of origin and followed the domicile of her husband in America.
