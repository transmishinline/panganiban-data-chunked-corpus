LOPEZ (OML), 93, was a man of honor, dignity, and integrity; he was courageous, patriotic, and quixotic. Many years ago, the First Philippine Holdings Corp. (FPH)—which he chaired—agreed to sell its concession in the North Luzon Expressway (NLEx) to Metro Pacific Investments Corp. (MPIC) chaired by Manuel V. Pangilinan.
