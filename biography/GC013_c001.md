From **1992 to 1995**, vacancies opened repeatedly in the Supreme Court. Each time, Art’s name was submitted to the **Judicial and Bar Council**. Each time, it was scrutinized. Each time, it was questioned. And each time, it was passed over. Seven times. Objections arose from the past — some vague, some petty, some resurrected from decades-old client dissatisfaction. A former case was reexamined.
