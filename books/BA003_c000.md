# Dress Her Down Anyway
Date:       2009-12-13
Publisher:  Philippine Daily Inquirer (Opinion)
Source:     With Due Respect (2011)
By: Artemio V. Panganiban Presidential Proclamation (PP) 1959 dated December 4, 2009 declaring “a state of martial law” and suspending the writ of habeas corpus in Maguindanao, is so inane and so baseless that even a law student will readily disown it.
