Worse, a rainstorm flooded the city. I had to wade in knee-deep water from the then FEU Hospital, where I was confined, to the University of the East, the exam venue. After what I thought was a lackluster first day, I wanted to quit the remaining exam days. But Dr. Salonga would not hear of it. With his encouragement, I still passed, copping the sixth highest place. Surrogate father.
