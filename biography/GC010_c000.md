**The Reckoning of a Year — and the Quiet Accumulation of Trust** When **June 1991** arrived, Art did not speak of legacy. He spoke of **accounting**. True to his nature, he refused to let the Rotary presidency dissolve into memory without measurement. At his valedictory meeting, he distributed a **94-page Final Report** — a document unprecedented in the history of the **Rotary Club of Manila**.
