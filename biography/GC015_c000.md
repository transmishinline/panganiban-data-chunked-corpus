**The Bench** In **October 1995**, **President Fidel V. Ramos** appointed Artemio Panganiban Jr. as **Associate Justice of the Supreme Court**. When Ramos later asked him how he had finally passed the Judicial and Bar Council, Art answered without irony: “I did not do it. The Lord did.” The bench changed him — and revealed him. He wrote relentlessly. Opinions poured from his chambers.
